Fixing the EOF: Bistability

Observations:

  1. Climate is either "summer monsoon" or "winter monsoon"

  2. Climate can’t be both! (except maybe in a transition month)

  3. Many climate phenomena will exhibit bistability: ENSO, NAO, AO, SAM

Fix:

  1. Divide the PC1/PC2 plane in two to halves

  2. Such that you minimize the variance of both halves

  3. The centroid of each half yield the "true" pattern
